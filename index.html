Chicken breast and potatoes topped with lemon-pepper seasoning and diced bell peppers and onions.


Description

One-pan roasted chicken and potatoes tossed in the most flavorful lemon spice combo and baked in the oven until juicy tender. Itâ€™s such an easy weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 6 potatoes, quartered
  • 2 green bell peppers, diced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 0.5 liter of water
  • 25% teaspoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon lemon-pepper seasoning

Directions

  1. Step

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step

    3. Place chicken breasts into a medium baking dish. Add potatoes, bell peppers, onion, water, and butter. Sprinkle lemon-pepper seasoning over the top.

  3. Step

    4. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken breasts are no longer pink in the centers and juices run clear, about 1 hour and 10 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
